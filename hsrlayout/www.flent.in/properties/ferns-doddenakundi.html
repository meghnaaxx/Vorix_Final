About this property

Nestled in the heart of Doddanekkundi, this luxury villa offers an unparalleled blend of sophistication and comfort within a prestigious gated society. The spacious home boasts an open-plan layout that elegantly merges modern design with timeless appeal, ensuring a light-filled ambiance throughout. One of its most alluring features is the meticulously curated garden—a verdant oasis that provides a perfect retreat for relaxation and outdoor entertainment, surrounded by mature trees and vibrant floral accents. Inside, every detail has been carefully considered, from high-end finishes to expansive living areas that cater to both intimate family moments and grand social gatherings. Residents benefit from exclusive amenities including round-the-clock security, a state-of-the-art clubhouse, and recreational facilities designed to enhance an elevated lifestyle. The villa’s prime location also ensures easy access to prominent tech parks and commercial hubs, making it an ideal choice for discerning professionals who value connectivity and convenience. This residence not only exemplifies luxurious living but also creates an inviting sanctuary where contemporary elegance meets serene, nature-inspired beauty, offering a truly exceptional living experience.
