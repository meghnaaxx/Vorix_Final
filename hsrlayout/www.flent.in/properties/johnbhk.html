About this property

Our well-equipped 1 BHK apartment offers a comfortable living space with essential amenities, including a cupboard, tea table, sofa, cot with an 8-inch branded mattress, pillow, curtains, and a shoe rack. Our services include housekeeping, laundry, mineral water, WiFi, a medical kit, and access to a terrace, making it an ideal home for a stress-free lifestyle.
